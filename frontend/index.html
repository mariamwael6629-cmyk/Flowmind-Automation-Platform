❓ FAQ

Frequently asked
questions

Do I need to know how to code?+
Not at all. FlowMind is designed for everyone — from developers to business users. Our visual canvas and AI assistant let you build complex automations without writing code. Developers can use our API and code-step nodes when needed.
How does the AI workflow generator work?+
Describe what you want in plain English. Our AI (GPT-4o) interprets your intent, picks the right apps and triggers, and builds the complete workflow. You review, adjust, and activate — usually in under 60 seconds.
What happens if a workflow fails?+
FlowMind has built-in error handling, automatic retries with exponential backoff, and detailed diagnostics. You get instant notifications, see exactly which step failed and why, and can replay failed executions once the issue is resolved.
Can I connect custom APIs and databases?+
Yes. FlowMind supports custom HTTP requests, webhooks, REST APIs, GraphQL, and direct database connections (Postgres, MySQL, MongoDB). You can create custom integrations with our SDK and publish them to your team library.
Is my data secure?+
Security is our top priority. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). We're SOC 2 Type II certified, GDPR compliant, and offer EU data residency. Enterprise plans include SSO, SAML, and full audit logs.
How is FlowMind different from Zapier or Make?+
FlowMind is the only platform with a built-in AI assistant that builds workflows from plain English, a professional-grade visual canvas with real-time animated data flow, and live execution intelligence with AI-powered diagnostics — not just logs.
